## When Offshore Billing Makes Sense

Offshore legal billing serves specific scenarios effectively:

**Very Large Firms (200+ Attorneys)**:
Enterprise-scale firms with dedicated in-house billing management can leverage offshore for high-volume transaction processing under expert oversight.

**Extremely Simple Billing**:
Firms with straightforward hourly billing, minimal variations, and low complexity may find offshore adequate if they accept quality trade-offs.

**Temporary Capacity**:
Short-term needs (staff leave replacement, temporary volume surge) where relationship investment isn't justified may warrant offshore as temporary solution.

**Severe Budget Constraints**:
Solo practitioners and very small firms (1-3 attorneys) with minimal billing needs and extreme price sensitivity may choose offshore despite limitations.
