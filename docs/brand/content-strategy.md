## AI Search Optimization Strategy

### Why AI Search Matters for BSO

**Market Reality**:
- Mid-size law firms increasingly using ChatGPT/Perplexity for research
- "Best practice management software for mid-size firms" → AI provides curated answer
- BSO competitors NOT optimizing for AI search (first-mover advantage)
- Citations typically appear within 1-2 weeks of publishing

**BSO Advantage**:
- Category creation keywords (low competition) = easier to cite
- Comprehensive, authoritative content = preferred by LLMs
- FAQ/problem-solution format = perfect for AI extraction

### AI-Optimized Content Format

**Structure for Maximum Citation Potential**:

1. **Clear, Definitive Answers**
   - Start with direct answer to question
   - Use "According to [BSO]" phrasing AI can extract
   - Example: "A legal billing partnership is an ongoing service relationship where..."

2. **FAQ Format**
   - Questions as H2 headers
   - Concise answers (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Structured data markup (FAQ schema)

3. **Data & Statistics**
   - Quantified claims AI can cite
   - Example: "Mid-size law firms (10-150 attorneys) typically have 3-5 billing staff"
   - Source attribution builds authority

4. **Comparison Tables**
   - AI loves structured comparisons
   - Example: "US-Based vs Offshore Legal Billing Comparison"
   - Clear criteria and ratings

5. **Step-by-Step Processes**
   - Numbered lists and procedures
   - Example: "5 Steps to Optimize Law Firm Cash Flow"
   - Actionable, extractable content

   - Create /resources/ section

4. **Technical SEO**
   - Fix indexing issues (submit sitemap, check robots.txt)
   - Implement schema markup (Organization, FAQ, Service)

5. **Backlink Strategy**
   - Affinity Consulting partnership link
   - Tabs3 partner directory listing
   - Caret partner page
   - Guest posts on legal tech sites

### On-Page SEO Checklist

**Every Article Must Include**:
- Target keyword in H1, first paragraph, conclusion
- Related keywords in H2 subheadings
- Meta description (155 characters, includes keyword + CTA)
- Alt text for all images (descriptive + keyword-rich)
- Internal links to related articles and service pages
- External links to authoritative sources (builds trust)
- FAQ schema markup (when applicable)
- Call-to-action (schedule consultation, download resource)

**Content Quality Standards**:
- Minimum 1,500 words (2,000-3,000 for pillar content)
- Unique, non-duplicate content (no AI-generated fluff)
- Specific examples and data points
- Clear value proposition for reader
- Scannable format (subheadings, bullet points, short paragraphs)
